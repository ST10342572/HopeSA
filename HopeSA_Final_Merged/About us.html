Overview

Hope SA Foundation is a well-established South African non-profit based in Johannesburg, Gauteng, founded around 2000. Our mission is to empower underserved communities by addressing poverty, hunger, education, healthcare, gender-based violence, and disaster relief. We align with the UN's Sustainable Development Goals and use an "Each One Help One" approach to meaningful community support

Mission

Our Mission is to equip educators and caregivers with the training and tools they need, advocate for improved standards, and ultimately build resilient communities for a brighter tomorrow.

Vission

The Hope Foundation SA was established with one purpose in mind: to bridge the gap between the current challenges faced by many ECD centres and the safe, quality environments our children need to flourish.

What we do

  • Food & Water Security: Provide meals, filtered drinking water, and emergency food relief—often feeding thousands during crises like floods, hunger emergencies, and winter drives.
  • Education & Skills Development: Supply school supplies, towels, stationery, and life skills, often on Exam season.
  • Gender-Based Violence Initiatives: We launched the “Safer Women Drive” providing care packs, support, and awareness programs in Johannesburg and KwaZulu-Natal.
  • Disaster Relief: We help victims of floods and international crises like Earthquakes, Floods, Drought.
